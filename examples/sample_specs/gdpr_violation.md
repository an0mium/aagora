# Customer Analytics Platform Specification

## Overview
A platform for collecting and analyzing customer behavior data to improve marketing effectiveness.

## Data Collection

### Data Points Collected
- Full name, email, phone number
- Home address and GPS location (continuous tracking)
- Browsing history across all partner sites
- Purchase history and payment methods
- Social media profiles and connections
- Health app data (steps, heart rate, sleep patterns)
- Device information and installed apps
- Voice recordings from customer service calls
- Facial recognition data from store cameras

### Data Sources
- Website cookies (permanent, no expiration)
- Mobile app background tracking
- Third-party data brokers
- Partner company data sharing
- Public social media scraping

## Data Processing

### User Profiling
- AI-generated personality assessments
- Predictive income and credit scoring
- Political affiliation inference
- Religious belief estimation
- Sexual orientation prediction
- Health condition risk scoring

### Data Sharing
- Marketing partners (500+ companies)
- Insurance companies for risk assessment
- Employers for background checks
- Government agencies upon request
- International subsidiaries in all countries

## User Controls
- Users can request data export (processed within 90 days)
- Deletion requests are logged but data is retained for analytics
- Opt-out applies only to future collection

## Data Retention
- All data retained indefinitely for model training
- Backups stored in multiple global regions
- No data minimization applied

## Consent Mechanism
- Pre-checked consent checkbox on signup
- Continued use implies consent to all processing
- Children's data collected with same mechanism

## International Transfers
- Data freely transferred to all countries
- No Standard Contractual Clauses needed
- US servers used for all EU customer data
